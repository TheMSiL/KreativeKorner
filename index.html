What We Offer

Safety First

At our center we always put safety first. 24/7 surveillance cameras and security guard on premises will let you feel safe and comfortable.

Creative Lessons and Workshops

Our center was established to inspire and engage minds by unleashing the creativity and potential.

Happy Environment

Our club is all about creating a positive and uplifting environment for you to enjoy.

A Perfect Learning Center for Your Kids

At Kreative Korner, we believe that every person is unique and has his/her own individual talents and abilities that need to be nurtured and encouraged. Our center offers a wide range of programs and activities that are designed to inspire creativity and imagination of all ages.

ART Lessons & Master classes in Kreative Korner

Cooking classes

Taking a cooking class is a great opportunity to learn about food, try new ingredients, and gain confidence in the kitchen while making tasty meals.

Learn more

Decoupage

Decoupage is a flexible and artistic craft that involves decorating surfaces like wood, glass, or metal with cut-out images, often from paper napkins or magazines, and sealing them with layers of varnish or glue.

Learn more

Epoxy Resin

Crafting with epoxy resin involves mixing two components - resin and hardener - to create a liquid mixture. This mixture, when cured, transforms into a solid, glossy surface that is perfect for coating different objects.

Learn more

Slime and Soap Making

Slime-making workshops are fun and colorful activities that are great for parties or creative gatherings. They bring excitement and collaboration among participants with vibrant colors, glitter, and themed add-ins.

Learn more

Chess Classes

Taking chess classes helps people learn the rules, tactics, and strategies needed to play chess. It also improves mental discipline and cognitive skills.

Learn more

Painting Classes

Painting classes provide a creative and supportive space for people to learn different painting techniques, styles, and materials, so they can express their artistic side.
